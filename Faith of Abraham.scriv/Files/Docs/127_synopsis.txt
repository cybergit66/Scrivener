The story of Abram’s decision to go to Egypt rather than remain in Canaan raises several themes for those of us who, like Abram, try to live as followers of God. It reminds us that even the heroes of our faith sometimes allowed themselves to be moved by circumstance rather than conviction.

Grigoni, M. R., Custis, M., Mangum, D., & Whitehead, M. M. (2012). Abraham: Following God’s Promise (Ge 12:10–20). Bellingham, WA: Lexham Press.
