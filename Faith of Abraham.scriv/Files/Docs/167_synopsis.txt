Gen. 11:27* is the beginning of the patriarchal story. Terah to be sure is not one of the patriarchs; he dies in Haran before the entrance into the land of Canaan. But as the father of Abraham, he belongs to the story that is to be told (R. Montague, La Civilization du Desert [1947]).
