Jesus exhorted His Jewish audience to model Abram by looking ahead to the fulfillment of God’s plan of salvation—a plan that Christ Himself fulfilled before their very eyes. Instead, the Jews opposing Jesus looked to the past, living by their tradition and idolizing Abram.

Grigoni, M. R., Custis, M., Mangum, D., & Whitehead, M. M. (2012). Abraham: Following God’s Promise (Ge 11:27–12:9). Bellingham, WA: Lexham Press.
