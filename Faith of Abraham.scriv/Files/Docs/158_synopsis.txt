Family and relatives—especially in the ancient world—provided love, care, acceptance, support, responsibility, training, education, and security for one another. This would have been true with Abram and his family and close relatives. It must have been very difficult for Abram to leave and forever separate himself from his loved ones. Yet this was exactly what God demanded of Abram.
